International Women's Day by Shafana

International Women's Day is a global day celebrating the social, economic, cultural and political achievements of women.It is annually marked on the 8th of March and celebrates women's achievements; raises awarness about women's equality and educates people on women's rights. International Women's Day has been occuring for over a century with the first event held in 1911 with poeple all over the world supporting women including women themselves.We celebrate women on this day and the progress that they have made towards gender equality.

Usually on Women's Day, poeple all over the world do all sorts of things to commerate this occasion such as hosting events or running a campaign or even launching a new intitiative. Schools also let children make posters and wear #IWD coloured clothes. You can also support Women's Day by donating to a female-focused charity or posting on social media on different achievements by women.
