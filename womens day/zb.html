Zenobia

Septimia Zenobia (240-275) was a third-century queen of the Palmyrene Empire in Syria who challenged the authority of the Roman Empire. She was not a commoner and was married to Odeaenathus. She conquered Egypt,Anatolia,Lebanon and Roman Judea until she was finally defeated by Aurelian, a Roman emperor.Many tales have recorded her fate and both her rise and fall has inspired historians,artists and novelists. She is a patriotic symbol in Syria

The first mention of Zenobia as queen was dated 2 to 3 years after the death of Odeanathus however she was designated as queen when her husband became king.Zenobia had also had accompanied her husband caimpaigns which led to her having boosted the morale of the soliders and gaininf political influence.
