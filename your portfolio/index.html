ABOUT

What are we?

'Your Portfolio' is a group of of designers and developers who work in social media designing, graphics, motion graphics, UI/UX, print design, typography, web-developing and app developing. We are here to help you build your online portfolio for education, buisness purposes or personal reasons.

What are the services we provide?

Since we have a wide range of designers, we can build you anything online such as a website for your brand or get you a personalized domain.You can contact us as per to what you need in order to build your online porfolio from a simple logo to multifunctional website. Our developers can also provide you serivces such as a mobile app design.

How do we carry out our design process?

When you usually contact us, we ask you if you want either a designer or developer. If you require a designer , then we ask you for which reason as in graphics, typography, motion-graphics, UI/UX, print design or typography. We then set up a meeting with the type of designer you require either online or offline(both our designers and developers prefer offline meetings to the convinience of time and location). If you are to choose offline, then when the designer has completed his/her work, they will give (as per your liking) a pendrive or the files online via the application of their choosing (as our designers dont all use the same application for the design process).When the designer is done with the project, they will ask for a final check to see if its to your liking, if not then they will make changes to the places you didnt like. On the other hand, if you are to choose wanting a developer then we ask you if you have a design ready or not. If not then you can either choose to hire one of our designers or you can hire a designer as per your liking. When the design is ready to be developed, we will ask you to choose a developer as per your liking (you can requst a meeting if you like). Once the developer is done, you can run a final check in with them to see if the project is to your liking or not.

For more information, you can use the information in the contact section to contact our managers.
